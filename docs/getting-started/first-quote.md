# Creating Your First Quote

Learn how to create a quote for a customer in Shop Director.

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:

- Customer information (name, phone, email)
- Vehicle details (year, make, model)
- Products or services to quote

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Start a New Quote

Click **Quotes** in the sidebar, then click the **New Quote** button.

<!-- SCREENSHOT: /quotes | Quotes list with New Quote button | highlight:a[href*="quotes/new"],.btn-primary -->

### 2. Add Customer Information

- Search for an existing customer, or
- Click **New Customer** to add someone new

<!-- SCREENSHOT: /quotes/new | New quote customer section | highlight:[name*="customer"],a[href*="customers/new"] -->

### 3. Add Vehicle

Enter the vehicle details:

- Year, Make, Model
- VIN (optional)
- Color (optional)

### 4. Add Line Items

Add products and services:

- Search your product catalog
- Enter custom items if needed
- Adjust quantities and pricing

### 5. Review and Send

Preview the quote, then either:

- **Save as Draft** - Continue editing later
- **Send to Customer** - Email the quote for approval

<!-- SCREENSHOT: /quotes/1 | Quote review with action buttons | highlight:input[type="submit"],.btn-success,.btn-primary -->

## What's Next?

Once the customer approves, you can [convert the quote to an order](../quotes/creating.md) and schedule the installation.

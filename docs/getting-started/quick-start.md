# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to Shop Director! This guide will help you get up and running in minutes.

## What is Shop Director?

Shop Director is a scheduling-first POS system designed specifically for 12-volt retail and installation shops. Whether you install car audio, security systems, remote starts, or lighting, Shop Director helps you manage quotes, schedule installations, and track your business.

## Your First Steps

### 1. Log In to Your Account

Navigate to [app.shopdirector.app](https://app.shopdirector.app) and sign in with your credentials.

### 2. Explore the Dashboard

Your dashboard shows today's schedule at a glance:

- **Upcoming appointments** - See what's scheduled for today
- **Recent quotes** - Quick access to quotes awaiting approval
- **Bay status** - Current installation bay availability

## Key Concepts

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Quote** | An estimate for a customer before they commit |
| **Order** | A confirmed quote ready for scheduling |
| **Work Order** | A scheduled job assigned to a bay and technician |
| **Invoice** | The final bill after work is complete |
